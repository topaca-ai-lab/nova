# Contributing to Nova

Thank you for contributing.

## Quality Bar

Please keep issues and PRs concise, technical, and reproducible.

### Issues

Include:

- clear problem statement
- minimal reproduction steps
- expected vs. actual behavior
- relevant logs/errors and environment details

### Pull Requests

Include:

- focused scope (one topic per PR)
- rationale and implementation notes
- tests or validation steps
- migration notes for breaking behavior

## Review Policy

- Maintainers triage all incoming issues and PRs.
- New contributors may receive guidance requests before detailed review.
- Low-quality, incomplete, or non-reproducible submissions may be closed.
